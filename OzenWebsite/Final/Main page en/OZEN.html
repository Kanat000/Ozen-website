ӨZEN Lounge Bar

REFRESH YOUR DAILY LUNCH RITUAL

Playing with texture, shape and light, Өzen offers a stylish, eclectic restaurant-lounge, where a careful focus on cooking manifests itself in vibrant flavors and beautiful presentation.

The Kazakh food menu includes organic seafood and meats, but the highlight is afternoon tea. Loose teas from around the world and coffee brewed by Chemex are served with three levels of sweet and savory snacks.

Kitchen

ӨZEN cuisine is the best European traditions, multiplied by modern technologies. In other words, a unique tandem of an oven soul and an open kitchen, so that every guest who comes to us to taste cabbage soup or stewed meat, could watch the cooking process through the glass.

Chef

Frank Koppelkamm is the chef of the ӨZEN restaurant. Frank is not only one of the best chefs in the world, but also an avid traveler, so the cuisine in the restaurant-house under his patronage really knows no boundaries. His culinary creations - an amazing mix at the junction of cultures from different countries and continents. Frank began his career in Bavaria back in 1988. He traveled in Asia with 2003, then moved to South America, where he honed his skills in Mexico and the Caribbean. By the way, there he is won the 5 Diamond Award at The Ritz-Carlton, Mexico. Soon, the master hit the road again and continued his career. in Indonesia, China and Singapore.

Chef - Confectioner

Godefroy Leinekugel Le Coq is a charismatic pastry chef who will delight our guests with his amazing desserts at festive Afternoon Tea! Godefroy's creations are incredible combinations, perfect shapes and breathtaking aromas!

You have the opportunity to see for yourself that the perfect desserts exist!

Technologies

Our dishes really "languish" in the oven, namely they are cooked over low heat for 6, 12 and even 18 hours! This undeservedly forgotten cooking method takes food from a new angle.

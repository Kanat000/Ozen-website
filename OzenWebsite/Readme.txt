About the project

and give more opportunity to order the foods or to book the table from the distance by applications.

Built With 
*Html
*Css
*Bootstrap
*Php
*Javascript
*Sql

Usage
You can see main information about our restourant in our main page;
You can see there our photos videos and some information about cooks;
If you want see the menu of our restaurant you can click "Menu" button and you can see;
If you want call or connect with our restaurant,
you can see it by clicking the button "Contacts" or by going footer of the website;
You can change the language of website by clicking the button "Language".
You can book the table by clicking button "Reserve".
